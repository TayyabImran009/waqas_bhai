Our Accreditations

Demonstrating Excellence in Security Service

Our accreditations aren’t just badges, they are proof of our commitment to doing things properly. Every standard we hold reflects our dedication to quality, compliance, and professionalism in everything we do. We work hard to maintain and exceed the benchmarks set by the UK security industry, ensuring our clients receive a service that’s not only dependable, but fully accountable.

SIA Approved Contractors

Regent FM currently hold SIA Approved Contractor Scheme status for the provision of Security Guarding.

When you choose an approved contractor, you can be sure that the business you are working with has passed a thorough quality assurance check.

ISO 9001 : 2015

ISO 9001 is the world’s most recognised quality management system standard, established by the International Organisation for Standardization (ISO). ISO 9001 helps Regent FM to implement clear, repeatable processes to maintain a quality service for their customers every time.

Safe Contractors

SafeContractor accreditation is recognised by clients and other SSIP schemes as it shows that Regent FM can demonstrate high levels of health and safety management, commitment to best practice in the work environment, and that the required levels of protection for our staff and contractors are firmly in place.

Construction Line Gold

Being one of verified member of Construction Line, enhances our client’s confidence saving them valuable time and money. Constructionline Gold illustrates to buyers that our organisation has been subjected to increased scrutiny around governance and risk management.

Acclaim SSIP

Acclaim is one of only four SSIP programmes in the UK that undertakes an impartial and independent assessment of an organisation’s health and safety documentation and work activities. This enhances confidence of our customers that Regent FM has the right systems and processes in place, and they can rely on us to complete a job to the highest standards.
