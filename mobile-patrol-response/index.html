Mobile Patrol & Response

Security doesn’t stop when you leave the site.

That’s where we come in.

At Regent FM, we provide professional Mobile Patrol and Response services that ensure your premises remain protected day and night. Our patrol officers deliver a visible deterrent, rapid response and complete peace of mind - wherever and whenever you need it.

Flexible, efficient and always alert, our mobile units are deployed to cover multiple locations or provide targeted support outside of standard hours. They carry out scheduled and random patrols, check perimeters, inspect access points, and verify alarm activations to ensure your property is always secure.

Fast response.
Visible presence.
Trusted protection.

Every patrol is backed by our in-house, 24/7 manned control room, which monitors all activity in real time. Our control operators maintain constant contact with officers in the field, coordinating rapid responses to alarms, incidents, or emergencies. This ensures that every situation is managed swiftly, safely, and professionally.

Our vehicles are clearly branded, GPS tracked, and equipped with modern communication systems for instant coordination. Every movement is logged and recorded, giving clients full transparency and detailed reporting after each patrol or response.

Whether you require nightly lock-ups, random mobile patrols, or full alarm response coverage, we tailor our services to meet your exact needs. Our flexible approach allows you to scale your protection efficiently, keeping costs manageable without compromising on quality.

Prevent. Protect. Respond.

That’s what Regent FM Limited delivers, professional mobile patrol and response services that keep your sites safe, your assets secure, and your operations running smoothly, 24 hours a day.
