Close Protection

Personal safety deserves more than reassurance - it deserves absolute confidence.

At Regent FM, we provide discreet, professional close protection services for individuals, families and organisations who require enhanced personal security. Our Close Protection Officers (CPOs) are experienced, highly trained professionals who operate with complete discretion, situational awareness, and respect.

Protection without intrusion.
Presence without disruption.

Every client’s circumstances are unique, and we tailor our approach accordingly. Whether you require full-time personal protection, secure travel arrangements, or short-term cover for events or business trips, our team provides a service built entirely around your lifestyle and schedule.

Our CPOs are licensed by the Security Industry Authority (SIA) and come from backgrounds in military, police, or specialist security roles. They are selected for their professionalism, discipline, and ability to make sound decisions under pressure. Their focus is on risk prevention, early threat identification, and maintaining a safe, comfortable environment for the client at all times.

Behind every Regent FM officer is our in-house, 24/7 manned control room, ensuring constant communication, oversight, and logistical support.
Real-time monitoring.
Rapid response.
Complete coordination.

This structure allows us to provide not just a bodyguard service, but a fully managed security operation - supported by intelligence, planning, and communication.

Confidentiality is central to everything we do. Our clients trust us to operate with discretion, sensitivity, and absolute professionalism. From executive protection to high-net-worth individuals and family security, we deliver a standard of service that reflects our core values: reliability, respect and reassurance.

Your safety is personal - and so is our service.

With Regent FM, you can depend on close protection that’s discreet, professional, and built entirely around you.
