ABOUT US

At Regent FM Limited, security isn’t just a service - it’s our commitment.

We exist to protect people, property, and peace of mind through professionalism, reliability and genuine care.

Founded on the belief that quality and accountability should never be optional, Regent FM has grown into a trusted provider of physical security services across the UK. From single-site guarding to large-scale operations, our focus remains the same; delivering dependable protection backed by real human values.

We believe great security is built on trust.
Trust in our people.
Trust in our standards.
Trust in our ability to deliver, every time.

Our officers are carefully selected, fully licensed, and trained to represent both Regent FM and our clients with professionalism and pride. They are supported by our in-house, 24/7 manned control room, ensuring constant communication, rapid response and complete operational oversight. It’s this structure that gives our clients total confidence in the service we provide.

We hold ourselves to the highest standards of compliance - exceeding industry requirements through regular audits, rigorous vetting, and continuous improvement. Our management team brings decades of experience in security operations, ensuring every client receives a tailored, proactive and fully compliant service.

Professionalism runs through everything we do. But so does personal attention. We believe in open communication, strong relationships and understanding each client’s unique environment. That’s why so many of our clients stay with us - because they know we deliver more than a service. We deliver assurance.

Reliable people.
Real accountability.
Round-the-clock protection.

That’s what defines Regent FM - a security company built on trust, experience and an unwavering commitment to doing things the right way.

Regent FM currently hold SIA Approved Contractor Scheme status for the provision of Security Guarding.
