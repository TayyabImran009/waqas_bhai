Front of House

Your front of house isn’t just an entry point. It’s the face of your business. It’s where first impressions are made.

At Regent FM, we understand that security officers in front-facing roles are more than guards, they’re ambassadors for your brand. Our officers combine professionalism, courtesy and vigilance to deliver a service that feels as welcoming as it is secure.

We handpick our Front of House Security Officers for their presentation, communication skills and confidence. Each is fully licensed and experienced in customer service and access management. They know how to greet visitors warmly and handle situations discreetly.

They protect.
They represent.
They reassure.

Every site is different, and so is every client. Whether we’re supporting a corporate reception, a residential concierge desk, or a public building, our officers adapt seamlessly to your environment; always reflecting your standards and values.

At Regent FM, customer service comes first. We believe effective security should never feel intrusive. Our officers are approachable, proactive and professional - the kind of people you’d want welcoming your guests. They manage visitor access, issue passes, handle enquiries and respond swiftly when needed, all while maintaining a reassuring presence.

Behind the scenes, our in-house 24/7 manned control room keeps every site connected and supported.
