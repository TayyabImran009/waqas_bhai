Construction

Construction sites are high-risk environments. Valuable equipment. Constant movement. Open perimeters.

At Regent FM, we understand that protecting construction sites demands vigilance, reliability and a proactive approach. Our Construction Security Officers are trained to manage the unique challenges of live projects - keeping people, property and progress safe around the clock.

We don’t just guard - we manage risk.

Our officers are fully licensed, experienced and trained to deal with trespass, theft, vandalism, and health and safety concerns. They patrol strategically, control access points and maintain accurate site records, ensuring that only authorised personnel enter restricted areas.

A visible security presence is one of the most effective deterrents against crime. But our officers go further- providing a professional, approachable presence that reassures site staff and contractors alike.

Behind every officer stands our in-house, 24/7 manned control room. It’s the operational heart of Regent FM.

Our control team provides real-time communication, incident monitoring and rapid response coordination, ensuring that every construction site is supported at all times. If something happens, we know immediately and act fast.

Each construction project is different and so is our approach. We tailor our security plans to your site layout, working hours, and risk profile. Whether it’s a long-term build or a short-term project, Regent FM delivers flexible solutions that adapt to your schedule and budget.

We also understand the importance of communication. Our officers work closely with site managers and contractors, integrating smoothly into daily operations to minimise disruption while maintaining maximum protection.

That’s what Regent FM Limited delivers - professional, compliant, and responsive construction security you can depend on, from the first day on site to project completion.
